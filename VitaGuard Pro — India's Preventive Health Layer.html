The Problem

India's healthcare system was
never built for prevention.

In India, 600 million people have never had a preventive health check-up — not because they don't care, but because the infrastructure doesn't reach them. By the time they see a doctor, it's already an emergency.

75%

of disease burden in India comes from conditions that are preventable — hypertension, diabetes, heart disease, and respiratory illness. All catchable early.

1:1456

Doctor-to-patient ratio in rural and semi-urban India. The WHO recommends 1:1000. The system is broken before it begins.

₹0

Annual preventive care spend for the average Tier-3 city resident. There is no family doctor, no annual check-up, no early warning system.

"I met Sunita last year — 42 years old, mother of three, ran a tailoring shop in Nagpur. She had a heart attack six months later. Her first health check was in the ICU. VitaGuard Pro was built because Sunita's story should have a different ending."

The Solution

India's first community-powered
preventive health platform.

🧠

AI Health Risk Assessment

Monthly 20-question validated protocol + optional camera-based vitals (heart rate via phone camera). Generates a personalized Prevention Score — a number users own, build, and improve over time.

No wearables needed
👩‍⚕️

₹49 Teleconsultations

Nurse-led video consultations at ₹49 — less than most OTC painkillers. Connected to 1,200+ verified healthcare professionals across 18 Indian states. Available in 9 regional languages.

₹49 per session
🤝

Community Health Agents

10,000+ trained local "VitaGuard Agents" — village-level entrepreneurs who onboard users, assist with assessments, and earn ₹3,000–8,000/month. CAC: ₹80 via this network.

Last-mile reach

Prevention Score API

Anonymized, consent-based health risk dataset licensed to insurance companies for underwriting — the real moat. Our B2B2C model creates a defensible data flywheel as users grow.
